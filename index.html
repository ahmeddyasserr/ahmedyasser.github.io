Cairo Book Fair 52

As a volunteer at the Cairo Book Fair, I had the opportunity to be a part of one of the most important events in the world of literature. This year's event was particularly significant as it was the first edition after the outbreak of the COVID-19 pandemic, and it was crucial for us to ensure the safety and wellbeing of our visitors. During my time as a volunteer, I played an important role in guiding and assisting visitors at the fair. Whether it was helping them navigate the different sections of the event, recommending books or providing information about specific authors, I was always on hand to offer my support. Overall, my experience as a volunteer at the Cairo Book Fair was incredibly fulfilling.
